Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and hashed password when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Usage data — basic session information such as login timestamps, used solely for security purposes.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data from your device beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate Spendly for you:

  • To display your expenses and spending summaries within the app.
  • To authenticate you securely and maintain your session.
  • To enable filtering, categorisation, and reporting features.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the Service described above.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are always stored as one-way hashes — we cannot read your password, and neither can anyone else.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. We may use the following limited third-party services to operate the app:

  • Hosting providers — to run the application infrastructure. These providers process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements.
  • Google Fonts — font assets are loaded from Google's CDN. No personal data is shared; only your IP address is exposed as part of a standard HTTP request.

We will never sell your data to advertisers or data brokers.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You may:

  • Access all your expense data at any time from within the app.
  • Edit or delete any individual expense record.
  • Delete your account entirely, which removes all your data from our systems.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this policy, please reach out to us through the app. We take privacy seriously and will respond promptly.

For details on the rules governing use of the Service, please also review our Terms and Conditions.
